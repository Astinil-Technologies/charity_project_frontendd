WHO ARE WE?

Since 2004, Bal Raksha Bharat has whole heartedly ensured that every child in India experiences the joys of a happy childhood and has access to opportunities for a better and secure future. Bal Raksha Bharat, since 2004 has whole heartedly ensured that every child in India experiences the joys of a happy childhood and has access to learning opportunities for a better and secure future. We firmly believe in fostering an environment wherein children are well-nurtured, with ample learning opportunities for growth and development in a protective environment. Through our work, we aim to support and contribute towards the Government of India’s vision to build a “Viksit Bharat” where every child thrives. Understanding the importance of child rights in India, we strive to ensure that every child is safeguarded and empowered. We sincerely believe that every child deserves the best that life has to offer, which is why we are committed to making sure they not just survive but flourish. We go above and beyond to save children because we have a strong sense of purpose and vigilante awareness. Bal Raksha Bharat, NGO operates on the ground in India across 15 states and 3 Union Territories. Our innovative programs cater to the special needs of children by providing them with a safe and healthy start, educational opportunities, and protection from harm. We are always among the first to react and the last to depart when a crisis arises. We tackle the most difficult problems affecting the children who are most difficult to reach, by drawing from a century of unparalleled experience. In addition to working with several government agencies, civil society organizations, and communities on a number of fundamental themes—Health and Nutrition, Education, Child Protection, Humanitarian Response, Disaster Risk Reduction, and Child Poverty—we also manage a number of programs throughout India. In line with Bal Raksha Bharat’s Global Breakthroughs for 2030, we work to create a future in which every child has the opportunity to live, be protected, and involved. Your donation for NGOs like ours plays a crucial role in making this vision a reality, and it may also qualify for donation exemption under applicable laws, adding another layer of impact to your contribution.
